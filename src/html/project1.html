PROJECT OVERVIEW

AI Hub is an internal enterprise tool designed to streamline AI-powered workflows within Colgate's organization.

This project involved designing a comprehensive interface that allows employees to access, manage, and utilize various AI tools and services in one centralized location.

The design focuses on user experience optimization, ensuring that complex AI operations are accessible to users of all technical levels.
