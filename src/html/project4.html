SPV Marketplace Project

UX/UI Design for Allocations Investment Platform

Project Overview

The SPV Marketplace project involved designing a comprehensive investment platform for Allocations, focusing on Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) management and investment opportunities.

This project required creating intuitive interfaces for investment tracking, portfolio management, and market analysis tools while maintaining compliance with financial regulations.

The design emphasizes clarity, trust, and ease of use for sophisticated financial users.
