Background

Colgate-Palmolive identified a need to increase employee engagement and productivity across their global workforce. Research showed that traditional performance management systems were not effectively motivating employees or fostering team collaboration. We developed a gamified platform that transforms routine work activities into engaging challenges, creating a more interactive and rewarding work environment.

User Research Insights Competitive Analysis

Our research revealed that employees were seeking more meaningful recognition and opportunities for growth. We analyzed existing gamification platforms in the enterprise space and identified key success factors: clear goal setting, social interaction, and meaningful rewards. This informed our design approach of creating a platform that balances fun with professional development.

Gamification design system components

Approach

We designed the platform around core gamification principles: clear objectives, progress tracking, and social interaction. The interface features intuitive dashboards that display individual and team achievements, leaderboards that foster healthy competition, and reward systems that recognize both individual contributions and collaborative efforts. We focused on creating a design that feels playful yet professional, appropriate for a corporate environment.

The high-fidelity design emphasizes clarity and engagement. The main dashboard provides an overview of current challenges, team rankings, and personal achievements. We implemented a clean, card-based layout that makes information easily digestible while maintaining visual interest through strategic use of color and typography. The challenge system allows users to participate in individual and team-based activities with clear progress indicators and reward previews.
