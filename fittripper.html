Background

Through extensive user research, we discovered that 75% of frequent travelers struggle to maintain their fitness routines while on the road. Users often face challenges finding suitable workout facilities, adapting their routines to limited equipment, and staying motivated without their usual support systems. This insight led us to develop Fittripper, a comprehensive solution that addresses the unique needs of fitness-conscious travelers.

Competitive Analysis User Research Insights

Our competitive analysis revealed that existing fitness apps lacked travel-specific features and often required extensive setup for each new location. Users were frustrated by the inability to quickly find nearby gyms, track workouts across different time zones, and maintain consistency in their fitness journey. We identified a significant gap in the market for a travel-focused fitness solution that could seamlessly integrate with users' nomadic lifestyles.

Piece of the Fittripper design system

Approach

We began with comprehensive user journey mapping, focusing on the various touchpoints where travelers interact with fitness during their trips. This phase allowed us to identify critical moments where the app could provide value, from pre-trip planning to post-workout tracking. Our design approach emphasized simplicity and speed, recognizing that travelers often have limited time and attention spans. We developed a modular design system that could adapt to different screen sizes and accommodate various workout types, from hotel room exercises to full gym sessions.

Moving into high-fidelity design, we focused on creating an intuitive interface that could be used quickly and efficiently. The design prioritized location-based features, with a prominent map interface for finding nearby fitness facilities. We implemented a streamlined workout tracking system that could handle various exercise types and equipment availability. The visual design emphasized clarity and accessibility, ensuring users could navigate the app easily even in unfamiliar environments or during rushed travel schedules.
